# Random review

For each random choice record:

- who owns the outcome;
- whether clients may know the candidate set;
- whether retries are possible;
- whether the result is persisted or replicated;
- how empty and invalid tables behave;
- how tests control the source;
- whether prediction can execute the choice more than once.

A random cosmetic animation can be client-local. A reward, damage result, price, target, or role assignment cannot.
